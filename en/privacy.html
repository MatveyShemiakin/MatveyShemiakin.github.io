Privacy and personal data processing policy

Effective 21 July 2026

1. General provisions

This Policy explains how personal data relating to visitors of matveyshemyakin.ru may be processed and protected. The Russian version governs compliance with Russian Federal Law No. 152-FZ on Personal Data.

The data operator is Matvey Yuryevich Shemyakin, acting as an individual. Privacy-related requests may be sent through Telegram: @ShemMYu.

2. Data that may be processed

  • technical website data, including IP address, device and browser type, date and time of access, page address, cookies and local-storage data;
  • pseudonymised or aggregated analytics data, only after consent where optional analytics is enabled;
  • name, Telegram username, phone number and other information voluntarily provided by a user when contacting the Operator through Telegram.

The website does not provide forms for submitting medical records or health information. Users should not send diagnoses, extracts, test results, photographs, treatment information or other special-category data through Telegram.

3. Purposes

  • operating, securing and maintaining the website;
  • remembering cookie and analytics preferences;
  • measuring website use and improving content, only with consent to optional analytics;
  • responding to organisational and general-information enquiries;
  • meeting applicable legal obligations.

4. Legal grounds

Processing may be based on consent, the need to provide and secure the website, the Operator’s legitimate interests in information security, and applicable legal requirements. Optional analytics should not start before consent is obtained.

5. Cookies and analytics

Essential cookies and local storage may be used to remember preferences and support website operation. Visitors may allow optional analytics or choose “Essential only”. The choice can be changed through “Cookie settings” in the website footer.

Where an analytics service is enabled, information may also be processed by that provider under its own terms. The Operator should configure analytics to minimise the amount of data collected and to comply with applicable requirements.

6. Telegram and external services

When a visitor follows a Telegram link, they leave this website. Further processing is also governed by Telegram’s own terms and privacy rules. Telegram is intended only for organisational and general-information enquiries. Medical advice, diagnosis and treatment are not provided through this website or Telegram.

7. Disclosure and retention

Data may be accessible to hosting providers, technical contractors and services supporting the website, only to the extent required for the relevant purpose. Disclosure to public authorities may occur where required by law.

Data is retained only for as long as needed for the stated purposes or legal obligations. It should then be deleted or anonymised unless another lawful basis requires continued retention.

8. User rights

Users may request information about processing, correction, restriction or deletion of their data and may withdraw consent. Requests can be sent through the contact above. The Operator may request information reasonably necessary to verify the requester’s identity.

9. Security

The Operator takes appropriate legal, organisational and technical measures to protect data against unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure, destruction and other unlawful actions.

10. Changes

This Policy may be updated when the website, connected services or applicable requirements change. The current version is available at https://matveyshemyakin.ru/en/privacy.html.
